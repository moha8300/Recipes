Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food dish made with marinated, spiced meat (chicken, beef, or lamb) thatâ€™s grilled or roasted, then wrapped in flatbread with fresh veggies and creamy sauces.

  • 500 g chicken thighs or breasts, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread or flatbread
  • Lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and pickles for filling
  • Garlic sauce or tahini sauce
  1. Mix yogurt, olive oil, garlic, and spices in a bowl. Add the chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour (overnight is better).
  2. Heat a skillet or grill pan and cook the marinated chicken until golden and fully cooked.
  3. Assemble the shawarma: place chicken strips on flatbread, add lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and pickles, and drizzle with garlic sauce or tahini.
  4. Slice the cooked chicken into strips.
  5. Wrap tightly and serve warm.
